Artist

She is a contemporary watercolor artist whose work explores the delicate balance between nature and emotion. Since 2019, Manasvi has been crafting vibrant floral compositions and abstract pieces that evoke both tranquility and curiosity. Each creation is a reflection of her meticulous attention to detail, her love for color, and her passion for bringing beauty into everyday life.
